Frequently Asked Questions

How do points work?

Completing tasks earns you XP and list points. You can spend those points to claim rewards you've set up on your list.

Can I share a list with my partner or family?

Yes, open the list settings and invite other users by email. They'll appear on your shared list once they accept.

What do daily and weekly lists do?

Lists set to daily or weekly automatically reset all tasks back to incomplete at the start of each new day or week, so recurring chores stay on top without any manual effort.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free plan lets you create up to 3 lists with 10 items each. Pro unlocks up to 10 lists, 30 items per list, larger sharing limits, and more.

How do I undo completing a task by accident?

After tapping a task complete, a toast will appear at the bottom of the screen with an Undo button. You have 3 seconds to tap it before the action is committed.
